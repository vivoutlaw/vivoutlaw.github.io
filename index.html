I am a research assistant in VISICS group, Electrical Engineering Department (ESAT) - Center for Processing Speech and Images (PSI), at the KU Leuven. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Luc Van Gool (KU Leuven/ ETH Zürich). I received my European Master-CIMET Degree in Multi/Hyper-Spectral imaging under the supervision of Prof. Alain Trémeau, Assoc. Prof. Javier Hernández-Andrés, Prof. Jon Yngve Hardeberg and Assoc. Prof. Şule Yildirim-Yayilgan at UJM, UGR and NTNU, campus GUC.

After finishing my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology, Pilani in 2011 and prior to my enrolment into the Masters, I have worked with Prof. Thomas Längle and Prof. Heinz Wörn at Fraunhofer IOSB and Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) as a research associate and a guest researcher.

My recent research work has been related to topics Multi/Hyper-Spectral Imaging, Computer Vision, Image Processing, Safe Human-Robot Collaboration, Machine Learning, Probabilistic Graphical Models and Augmented Reality.

Past european funded projects that I have contributed to are: CADaVISION, ViMuDat and AMIKA. Presently working on ROVINA.

 If you need to contact me, the best way to reach me is via email or by skype.
